### Camera and Gallery Access

The Application may request access to your **camera** or **photo gallery** to support AI-powered features, including the **Virtual Christmas (Christmas AI)** mode.  

This feature allows users to take or upload photos to create **AI-generated Christmas-themed images** (for example, festive backgrounds, Santa Claus, reindeers, gifts, or decorations).

- Photos are processed **temporarily** and **not stored permanently** on any external server.

- All photo processing is **user-initiated**, and original images are automatically deleted after generation.
